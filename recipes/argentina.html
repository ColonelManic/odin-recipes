Dulce De Leche

Description

Dulce de leche (or milk jam), is produced by the simmering of milk and sugar. It is very popular in Argentina.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour two tablespoons of water into the bottom of a saucepan and thoroughly wet the bottom of the pan.
  2. Pour in the milk, baking soda and sugar.
  3. Heat on a medium heat, while stirring to dissolve the sugar.
  4. When it begins to boil, lower the heat and simmer, stirring occasionally for about 45 minutes.
  5. At this point, the preparation should have thickened.
  6. Continue cooking, stirring very frequently until the mixture thickens and turns golden brown.
  7. Stop cooking when the consistency suits you. However, make sure the mixture does not to get too thick as it hardens a little while cooling.
  8. Pour the jam into a glass jar.
  9. Dulce de leche can be kept in the refrigerator.
